Fieldwork data collection made easy

Design your own sampling sheets with date/time, text, number, location and photo inputs. Organise work into campaigns and sample sets, and export CSVs for instant analysis.

Explore Features Get Support

Custom sampling sheets

Create forms that match your project: free‑text notes, numeric fields (with units), date/time, GPS location, and photos.

Works fully offline

No account required, no background syncing. All data stays on your device until you choose to export it.

Camera & photo library

Capture photos in-app or attach from your library. Photos link directly to their sample in the CSV export bundle.

Fast CSV export

Export a whole campaign, a sample set, or a single sample. CSVs are packaged with photos in a single .zip for easy sharing.

How FieldApp organizes your work

FieldApp uses a simple hierarchy so large projects stay manageable.

Campaigns

Top‑level projects. Each campaign can contain many sample sets (e.g., a field season, expedition, or study area).

Sample Sets

Group related samples by day, site, or sub‑project. Keep navigation clear as your dataset grows.

Samples

The individual records you collect—notes, numbers, timestamps, GPS coordinates, and multiple photos per sample.

Export for analysis

Exports are standard CSVs compatible with Excel/Numbers and analysis tools like R or Python. If photos are included, they’re bundled in a .zip with clear links back to each sample.

Privacy & data control

Data lives on your device until you export. No hidden uploads or third‑party storage.

Typical uses

Environmental surveys, glaciology, ecology, geology, archaeology, hydrology, education, lab sampling, and any project that benefits from structured field notes.
